Clovelly

Clovelly offers a little something for everyone. It is a special place where you can unwind with friends, take time for yourself, raise a glass to mark an important occasion or indulge in activity.

We are living in a very different time. Some things at Clovelly are changing – but our commitment to value and quality is stronger than ever. We feel privileged to be able to open and welcome our customers. Our team is working hard to provide a haven where our guests can retreat to relax and enjoy.

Clovelly 2020 will feel a little different – but we think you will like what you find.

We look forward to seeing you soon.

The Clovelly Team
